## [Correlated Randomness and PRNG Implementation in Slay the Spire 2](https://tck.mn/blog/correlated-randomness-sts2/) ⭐️ 8.0/10

The article details the technical implementation of correlated randomness and Pseudo-Random Number Generators (PRNGs) in Slay the Spire 2, addressing challenges like ensuring cross-platform consistency. This approach aims to provide a more controlled and predictable random experience compared to relying on standard library implementations. This technical deep-dive is significant for game development as it addresses critical software engineering challenges like ensuring consistent randomness across various platforms, which directly impacts game fairness and player experience. A robust PRNG implementation is crucial for roguelike games where randomness is a core mechanic. Slay the Spire 2 implements its own PRNG within the codebase to guarantee seed consistency across platforms, unlike the first game where standard library differences caused discrepancies. However, the game's use of a 32-bit hash function for seeds effectively limits the total number of unique seeds to 4 billion, a reduction from the original game's 64-bit seeds.

hackernews · rdmuser · 6월16일 09:46 · [커뮤니티 토론](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48552844)

**배경 지식**: Pseudo-Random Number Generators (PRNGs) are algorithms that produce sequences of numbers that appear random but are entirely determined by an initial "seed" value, meaning the same seed always yields the same sequence. Correlated randomness in game design refers to the practice of linking multiple random events to a single underlying random sequence, which helps maintain a consistent game experience and prevents extreme statistical outliers.

**커뮤니티 토론**: The community discussion highlights the benefits of in-code PRNGs for cross-platform consistency and seed stability, contrasting with the original Slay the Spire's issues. Concerns were raised about the new 32-bit seed hash limiting unique seeds to 4 billion, which could make "RNG hell" scenarios easier to prove but reduce the variety of "high roll" runs.

## [Apple's Vehicle Motion Cues Feature Alleviates Car Sickness](https://www.theverge.com/tech/942854/apple-vehicle-motion-cues-review-really-work) ⭐️ 7.0/10

Apple has introduced a new iOS 18 feature called 'Vehicle Motion Cues,' which displays animated dots on the edges of a device's screen to visually represent vehicle motion. The author of the article reported significant relief from car sickness after using this feature. This feature is significant as it offers a novel, accessible solution to the common problem of motion sickness, potentially improving the quality of life for many users. It highlights Apple's continued focus on user experience and accessibility, addressing a long-standing discomfort with a simple yet effective visual aid. The 'Vehicle Motion Cues' feature, part of iOS 18, works by overlaying animated dots on the screen's edges that move in sync with the vehicle's motion, detected by internal sensors like accelerometers and gyroscopes. This visual feedback helps to reduce the sensory conflict between what the eyes see and what the inner ear feels, which is a primary cause of motion sickness.

hackernews · neilfrndes · 6월16일 16:12 · [커뮤니티 토론](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48557530)

**배경 지식**: Motion sickness occurs when there's a mismatch between the sensory information received by the eyes and the inner ear, which detects balance and motion. For example, when reading in a car, your eyes perceive a stationary environment (the book or screen), while your inner ear senses the vehicle's movement, leading to confusion in the brain that can manifest as nausea.
