## [Valve Launches Steam Machine with Open Customization and Fair Reservation System](https://store.steampowered.com/news/group/45479024/view/685257114654870245) ⭐️ 9.0/10

Valve has officially launched its new gaming hardware platform, the Steam Machine, which emphasizes open customization and features a novel, user-friendly randomized reservation system. This launch introduces a new option for PC gaming with a focus on user freedom and fair access. This launch is significant as Valve, a major player in the gaming industry, is introducing a hardware platform that promotes an open ecosystem, allowing users to install other operating systems and applications. The innovative randomized reservation system also aims to set a new standard for product launches, prioritizing fairness over speed. A core aspect of the Steam Machine is its open hardware philosophy, allowing users to install their own applications or even alternative operating systems, contrasting with many locked-down gaming consoles. The reservation system employs a randomized order over several days to prevent bots and quick-refreshing users from monopolizing initial stock.

SteamOS 3.0, based on Arch Linux, includes Valve's Proton compatibility layer, enabling many Windows games to run on Linux.

## [Moebius: 0.2B Inpainting Model Claims 10B-Level Performance](https://hustvl.github.io/Moebius/) ⭐️ 8.0/10

Moebius is a new 0.2 billion parameter image inpainting model that claims to achieve performance comparable to much larger 10 billion parameter models, offering significant efficiency gains for computer vision tasks. This development is significant for AI efficiency, as it could make high-quality image inpainting more accessible and reduce computational resource requirements for various computer vision applications. Moebius is a 0.2 billion parameter model, a fraction of the size of 10 billion parameter models it claims to rival, though community discussions highlight limitations such as visibly smoother inpainted regions, poor handling of novel objects, and a 512x512 output resolution limit.

The number of parameters in an AI model generally indicates its complexity and capacity, with larger models typically requiring more computational resources but often achieving higher performance.

Canada has announced an ambitious plan to construct up to 10 new nuclear reactors within the next 15 years, leveraging its domestic capabilities and resources to meet future energy demands. This initiative is significant as it represents a major national energy infrastructure investment, aiming to secure Canada's future energy supply and potentially impacting its broader tech and industrial ecosystems. The plan emphasizes utilizing Canada's domestic capabilities, including its large uranium reserves and experience with CANDU reactor design and refurbishment, to support the construction of up to 10 reactors over the 15-year period.

hackernews · geox · 6월22일 19:06 · [커뮤니티 토론](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48634585)

**배경 지식**: Nuclear energy provides a reliable, baseload power source, crucial for balancing intermittent renewable energy sources like solar and wind. CANDU (CANada Deuterium Uranium) reactors are a Canadian-developed pressurized heavy-water reactor design known for their ability to use natural uranium and on-power refueling.

**커뮤니티 토론**: Community discussion is mixed, with some users supporting the plan due to Canada's domestic uranium reserves, CANDU reactor expertise, and the need for baseload power.
