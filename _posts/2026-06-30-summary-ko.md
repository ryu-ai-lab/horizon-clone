## [US Supreme Court Rules Geofence Warrants Require Constitutional Protections](https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2026/jun/29/supreme-court-geofence-warrants-case-decision) ⭐️ 9.0/10

The US Supreme Court has ruled that law enforcement must obtain a warrant based on constitutional protections to access geofence data. This decision significantly impacts digital privacy and police investigative methods. This ruling is a groundbreaking legal development that strengthens digital privacy rights for individuals, setting a new precedent for how law enforcement can utilize location data in investigations. It will likely influence future technological developments and legal frameworks concerning data collection. The ruling mandates that warrants for geofence data must meet constitutional standards, specifically impacting how law enforcement requests location information from companies like Google, which previously provided data in multiple tranches. This means police can no longer broadly request data without demonstrating probable cause for specific individuals or locations.

## [Rocket Lab Acquires Iridium for Fully Integrated Space Company](https://investors.rocketlabcorp.com/news-releases/news-release-details/rocket-lab-acquire-iridium-historic-deal-creating-fully) ⭐️ 9.0/10

Rocket Lab has announced its acquisition of Iridium, a significant move aimed at creating a fully integrated space company by combining Rocket Lab's launch and satellite manufacturing capabilities with Iridium's established satellite constellation and valuable spectrum. This acquisition represents a major strategic step towards vertical integration in the space industry, potentially reshaping market dynamics by allowing Rocket Lab to control more aspects of the space value chain, from manufacturing and launch to satellite operations and services. The deal combines Rocket Lab's expertise in small satellite launches and manufacturing with Iridium's profitable LEO satellite constellation and its allocated L-band spectrum, providing Rocket Lab with a guaranteed customer base and critical communication infrastructure.
